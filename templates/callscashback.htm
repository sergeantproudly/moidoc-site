Idea

The main goal of this project was to help mobile operator customers to control their account, including data traffic usage, rapid balance recharge. The app should also provide a convenient solution for users to make international voice calls.

Process

Our team took on the job of market research, visual design and development. We have worked through dozens of concepts to come up with a clean and efficient design for the mobile app. We were able to build iOS and Android versions within just 2 months.

International calls

Our voice call APIs create new lines of communication through VoIP & SIP integration for clear voice communication.

Make free international voice calls from the app to landline and mobile numbers.

Internet and tariffs

The client can access tariffs for internet data and calls inside the app, as well as traffic usage for the period.

Accounts can be topped-up with a credit card in a matter of seconds.
