Idea

The main goal was to help mobile users get the most value out of their mobile plans by allowing them to sell their unused SMS messages. The solution our team came up with makes mobile communication cheaper and more convenient for the customer.

Process

Our process was focused on identifying easy acquisition flows, generating value early to retain users, and building a solid infrastructure for scalability essential for such projects. The development team was then able to release the MVP version in a month.

Statistics & tracking

Clear and informative statistics lets users better track passive income and keep enough messages for emergency purposes.

We believe that cashback apps should be easy to use and honest with their users.
