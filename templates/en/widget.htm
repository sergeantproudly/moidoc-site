Online patient scheduling

MD’s web widget increases conversion by allowing patients to schedule appointments right from clinics’ website, social media profiles, or even through a link in the outgoing emails

Mobile patient scheduling

    • Accept online bookings

      Patients can book their appointments via multiple channels like clinics’ website, mobile app, Facebook, Instagram or even Google Business Profile

    • Reduce cancellations

      Reduce cancellations and no-shows by as much as 30% by sending patient appointment reminders via text, email, or mobile notifications

    • Gather client information

      Customised intake forms gather client information during the scheduling process. Client can set up texts & digits, checkboxes or date responses

    • Accept online payments

      Patients can enjoy a seamless experience all the way from scheduling a visit through client checkout through a range of payment processors

    • Automate reporting

      Customisable reports help monitor the business, identify and prevent problems, see ways for improvement, and support future growth

    • Custom integrations & API

      Native integration with Facebook, Instagram, Google My Business, custom integrations with Wordpress and other relevant CMS systems
